Hi, nice to meet you!

My name is Sabrina Tionloc.

I am a recent graduate from The University of North Texas. I have a bachelors in Visual Art Studies and two minors in French and Art History. I began my collegiate career with the intention of becoming an Art Educator, but once my senior year hit, I realized that becoming an Art Educator was not my end goal. I was on a journey to seek out a deeper passion and another direction. I was encouraged by my father to try a coding bootcamp, so I enrolled in a front-end development workshop called SheCodes. Within two weeks, I was enthralled by the content and the process of learning computer programming languages. Middway through my intro course, I made the decision to enroll in the 6 month intensive workshop through SheCodes.

Today, I am wrappng up the final weeks of my SheCodes workshop and am continuing to teach myself the complexities of JavaScript, HTML, CSS, and other front-end oriented programs and languages with the ultimate goal of landing a front-end development job.
